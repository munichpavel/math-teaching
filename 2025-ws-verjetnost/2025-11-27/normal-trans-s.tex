\subsubsection*{A solution}

Let $z = x/5 - 9/5$. Then $Z \sim N(9/5 - 9/5, 25/25) = N(0, 1)$.

We calculate next, using $X = 5Z + 9$:
\begin{align*}
P(X \leq 0) &= P(5 Z + 9 \leq 0) \\
&= P(Z \leq -9/5) \\
&= 1 - P(Z < 9/5) \\
&= 1 - P(Z \leq 9/5) \text{\, as }\{Z = 9/5\}\text{has measure 0} \\
&= 1 - \Phi(9/5),
\end{align*}
since $Z\sim N(0,1)$.
